Trains

In 1864, the Governor General of the Dutch East Indies, Sloet Van Beele, held a ceremony to build the first railroad connecting the cities of Semarang and Surakarta. The first company to start a railway construction project was the Nederlands-Indische Spoorweden Maatschappij (NISM). Today, the Kereta Api operates extensive routes across Java.
